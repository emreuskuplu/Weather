About WeatherApp
You can see weather of your location and you can add bookmarks with google map to see their weather.
You can see detailed weather and forecast weather days of your location or bookmarks.

Using WeatherApp
Tip 1: Your location weather at top bookmark list of home page.

Tip 2: You can open the map via ( ) button for adding bookmarks.

Tip 3: When you are on map, you can adding bookmarks with keep pressing 2 seconds.

Tip 4: You can refresh weather via ( ) button.

Tip 5: You can search bookmarks with their location name via magnifying glass button.

Tip 6: You can open your location detailed weather and forecast weather days via click your location weather image or temp degree.

Tip 7: You can open your bookmarks detailed weather and forecast weather days via click atop bookmarks in list.

Tip 8: When you are on detailed weather, you can see detailed weather of forecast days via click atop them.
